Non-Negotiable

The Rules That Make It Work

Double Progression — The Engine
Every exercise has a rep range. When you hit the top of the range on ALL sets with clean form, add weight next session. That's the entire system. Log every session — without the log, there is no progression, just exercise.
Effort Standard: 1–2 Reps From Failure
You're in a surplus now — recovery supports real effort. Every working set ends 1–2 clean reps shy of failure. If you could have done 4 more reps, the set didn't count.
Low Volume Is Intentional
Mostly 2 working sets per exercise — Jack's entire framework runs on this. 2 sets done brutally hard with progression beats 4 sets of pacing. Do not add sets. Add weight, add reps, add intensity.
Fatigue Checkpoint
If two consecutive sessions show strength regression on your logged lifts, cut volume 30% for 4–5 days, sleep more, then resume. Regression is data, not failure.
Zone 2: Capped at 2–3 Sessions/Week
Cardio is now a health tool, not a fat loss tool. Daily fasted cardio eats the surplus you're trying to build with. Keep 2–3 Zone 2 sessions for heart health and recovery — no more.
Daily Vacuums — Unchanged
3 rounds every morning, fasted, exactly as you've been doing. Not part of gym sessions. The transverse abdominis work continues through the bulk to keep the waist tight as the body grows.
