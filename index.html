Meal Prep
First 30 days rule: No training to failure. Stop 2–3 reps shy every set. Slow eccentrics (2–3 sec lowering phase). Before each set ask yourself: "What muscle am I about to use?" Build the connection first — the load follows. Progressive overload starts Week 2 — add small weight or one rep per week where form allows.
Green dot = Jack's Priority Exercise. These are the 10 exercises from Jack's framework woven throughout every session. They take priority position in each workout — primaries first, accessories after.

Upper A — Traps, Back, Rear Delt

Primary target: Traps & upper back thickness. Initiate every pull with the traps. This session sets the foundation for your masculine upper body silhouette.
Exercise Sets Reps Key Cue

Rack Pull
Smith machine or squat rack

Protein + fat dominant. Eggs, ground beef, salmon, or any combination. Bone broth. This is your biggest meal — you've been fasted 18–20 hrs, protein synthesis is primed. Eat enough here to cover most of your daily protein target.
3–4 PM Meal 2 — Main (Training Day Carbs Here)
